HOW UNDERWRITE WORKS

Small settlement spine, clear failure modes.

  1. Trusted evidence comes in.An oracle or evidence source signs a risk observation.
  2. The agent verifies it.The Rust agent checks signature integrity and calculates deterministic payout.
  3. The contract checks rules.Casper enforces policy existence, claimant, oracle, freshness, replay, and payout bounds.
  4. The vault pays valid claims.A valid attestation records settlement and releases the bounded CEP-18 payout.
  5. Bad claims are rejected.Duplicate and stale claims produce explicit failed Testnet transactions.
